Pre-Ride Preparation

Don't miss a
single thing.

EscapeArc's complete packing list — gear, documents, medications, bike prep. Built from experience across 150+ expeditions.

📄 ID & Travel Documents
Valid Govt ID (Voter/Passport/Aadhaar/DL) — PAN NOT valid
Driving Licence (valid for bike class)
Vehicle RC Book
Insurance Certificate
PUC Certificate
3 Passport size photos
High-altitude fitness medical certificate
🎒 Bags & Gear
60L rucksack (main bag)
15–20L day pack / tank bag
2L water bottle / hydration pack
Power bank (20,000 mAh+)
Head torch + spare batteries
Spare bike key
Waterproof dry bags
Rain cover for bags
👕 Clothing
Thermal base layers (top + bottom)
Rain gear (jacket + pants)
2 woollen sweaters / fleece
2 long pants
4 pairs socks + inner socks
Ankle shoes for off-bike
💊 Medications & First Aid
Altitude sickness tablets (consult doctor)
Headache tablets
Diarrhoea / stomach medicine
Motion sickness tablets
Cough relief tablets
Antiseptic cream + bandages
Crepe bandage + sprain spray
Glucose powder / ORS
Personal prescription medicines
First aid kit
Sunscreen SPF 50+ (high-altitude UV)
Moisturiser, lip balm, cold cream
🏍️ Riding Gear (Mandatory)
ECE/DOT/ISI/SNEL certified helmet — full-face or modular ONLY
Half-face / crash helmet: NOT ALLOWED
Riding jacket with CE approved armour
Riding gloves with knuckle protection
CE certified riding pants (recommended)
Riding boots with ankle protection
Visor cleaning kit + spare visor
Anti-fog cream/spray
🔧 Bike Pre-Ride Checklist
Chain stretch + sprocket wear
Wheel alignment + tyre pressure + tread
Headlight + tail + signals
Rear swing-arm bush
Clutch + cable condition
All cables (replace if frayed)
Suspension check
Engine oil level
Air filter clean
Replace fuel filter
Brake pads — check 2–3 times
Full service at authorised centre before tour
💡 Ride Guidelines:
  • Fuel tanks must be full before each day's ride
  • Carry hard cash — no ATMs in many Spiti/Ladakh spots
  • No solo-riding without Road Captain's GO signal
  • Max luggage: 10 kg/person on bike. Extra = ₹500/kg
  • No trolley bags — rucksacks only on all EscapeArc tours
